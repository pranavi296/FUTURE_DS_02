#  Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Dashboard

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data using **Power BI** to understand business performance and identify important patterns in sales and profit.

The dashboard converts raw transaction data into meaningful visual insights that help answer practical business questions such as which products generate the most revenue, which regions perform best, and how discounts impact profitability.

The goal of this project was to practice **data analysis, business intelligence, and data visualization skills** by building an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision making.


##  Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify high-performing and low-performing product categories.
* Compare regional sales and profit distribution.
* Understand how discounts affect profit margins.
* Study sales trends over time.
* Build a clear and interactive dashboard for business insights.


##  Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes multiple visual components to explore the data from different perspectives.

### 🔹 KPI Summary

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin

These indicators provide a quick overview of business performance.

### 🔹 Sales Analysis

* Sales by Category
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Monthly Sales Trend

This helps understand which products drive revenue.

### 🔹 Profitability Analysis

* Profit by Category
* Profit by Region
* Discount vs Profit analysis

This section highlights areas where profitability can be improved.

### 🔹 Regional Performance

* Sales comparison across different regions
* Geographic distribution of sales and profit


##  Key Insights

* Technology category contributes the highest revenue compared to other categories.
* Some categories generate strong sales but lower profit margins due to higher discounts.
* Regional performance varies significantly, with certain regions contributing more to total profit.
* Discounts have a noticeable impact on profitability.
* Sales patterns fluctuate across months, indicating seasonal trends.
